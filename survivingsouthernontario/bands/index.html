Metric
Like most things from Toronto and the indie genre this band is amazing. The smooth voice of their lead vocalist/synthesizer/guitarist/hot dog stand owner will lull yuo into a state similar to nirvana. The lyrics and melodies will astound you and keep you coming back for more. Simply stunning work comes from this group, a must listen, even if its just a taste (hey they were in plenty of things, including Scott Pilgrim)

I will note that a lot of great heavy bands orignate from Canada you just have to find them.
